Japan

Japan's women's rugby team is known for its speed, skill, and tactical awareness. The team has made significant progress in recent years, competing in the World Cup and Asian championships. Japan's players are celebrated for their discipline, teamwork, and innovative style of play.

With a growing rugby culture and strong support from fans, Japan is ready to make an impact at the 2025 World Cup. The team is focused on inspiring the next generation of female rugby players in Asia and beyond.
