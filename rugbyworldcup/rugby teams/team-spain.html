Spain

Spain's women's rugby team has made great strides in recent years, competing in European and world tournaments. The team is known for its technical skills, creativity, and commitment to growing the sport in Spain. Spanish players are celebrated for their teamwork and tactical awareness.

With a strong rugby culture and growing support, Spain is ready to make an impact at the 2025 World Cup. The team is focused on inspiring young players and expanding the reach of women's rugby in Spain.
