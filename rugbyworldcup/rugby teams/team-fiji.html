Fiji

Fiji's women's rugby team is renowned for its speed, agility, and Pacific Island flair. The team has made impressive progress, competing in regional and world tournaments. Fiji's players are known for their attacking style and creative play, making them exciting to watch.

With a strong rugby tradition and passionate support, Fiji is ready to challenge the world's best at the 2025 World Cup. The team is focused on inspiring young players and growing the women's game in the Pacific.
