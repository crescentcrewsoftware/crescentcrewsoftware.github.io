England

England is the host nation for the Women's Rugby World Cup 2025. With a rich history in women's rugby, England has consistently been one of the top teams in the world, winning the World Cup in 2014 and finishing as runners-up several times. The team is known for its strong forward play, tactical kicking, and passionate fan base. England's players are among the most skilled and experienced, and the team is expected to perform strongly on home soil. The squad features a blend of seasoned internationals and exciting young talent, making them a favorite to lift the trophy in 2025.

England's home matches will be played at Twickenham Stadium, the largest dedicated rugby stadium in the world. The team is led by a world-class coaching staff and supported by a vibrant rugby community. England's commitment to developing women's rugby at all levels has made them a powerhouse in the sport.
