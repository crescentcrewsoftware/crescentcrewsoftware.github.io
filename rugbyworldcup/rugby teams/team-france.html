France

France is a perennial contender in the Women's Rugby World Cup, known for their flair, creativity, and passionate support. The French team has reached the semi-finals more than any other European nation and is respected for its disciplined defense and attacking style. France's players are celebrated for their technical skills and ability to play expansive rugby.

French rugby culture is deeply rooted in tradition, and the national team enjoys strong backing from fans across the country. The squad features a mix of experienced internationals and rising stars, making France a team to watch in 2025. Their matches are always exciting, with a focus on teamwork and tactical innovation.
