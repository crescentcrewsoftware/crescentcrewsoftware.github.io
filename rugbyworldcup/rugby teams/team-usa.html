USA

The USA Eagles were the first ever Women's Rugby World Cup champions in 1991. The team is known for its athleticism, determination, and pioneering spirit. USA has a strong tradition in women's rugby and continues to develop talented players through its collegiate and club systems.

With a focus on speed and power, the Eagles are always a threat in international competition. The team is supported by a growing fan base and a commitment to expanding the sport across the United States.
