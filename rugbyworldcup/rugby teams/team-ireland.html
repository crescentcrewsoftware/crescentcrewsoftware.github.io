Ireland

Ireland's women's rugby team has a proud history, including hosting the World Cup in 2017. The team is known for its tenacity, skill, and tactical awareness. Ireland has produced many talented players who have excelled in both domestic and international competitions.

With a strong rugby tradition and a commitment to growing the women's game, Ireland is poised to challenge the best teams in the world at the 2025 tournament. The team is supported by enthusiastic fans and a vibrant rugby community.
