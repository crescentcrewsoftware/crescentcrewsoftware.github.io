Canada

Canada is one of the strongest teams in North America, having reached the final of the Women's Rugby World Cup in 2014. The Canadian team is known for its physicality, resilience, and commitment to the sport. Canada has produced many world-class players who have excelled on the international stage.

With a focus on grassroots development and a strong rugby culture, Canada continues to be a force in women's rugby. The team is supported by passionate fans and a dedicated coaching staff, making them a tough opponent for any side in the tournament.
