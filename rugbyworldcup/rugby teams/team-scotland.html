Scotland

Scotland's women's rugby team is known for its fighting spirit and historic rugby heritage. The team has made significant progress in recent years, competing in the Six Nations and World Cup tournaments. Scotland's players are celebrated for their skill, determination, and teamwork.

With a strong domestic league and growing support for women's rugby, Scotland is ready to take on the world's best at the 2025 World Cup. The team is focused on building for the future and inspiring young players across the country.
