Brazil

Brazil's women's rugby team is known for its energy, flair, and South American spirit. The team has made significant progress, competing in continental and world tournaments. Brazilian players are celebrated for their athleticism, creativity, and commitment to the sport.

With a growing rugby culture and strong support, Brazil is ready to make an impact at the 2025 World Cup. The team is focused on inspiring young players and expanding the reach of women's rugby in Brazil and South America.
