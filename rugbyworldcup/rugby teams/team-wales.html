Wales

Wales is renowned for its passionate rugby culture and proud tradition. The Welsh women's team has made significant strides in recent years, competing fiercely in the Six Nations and World Cup tournaments. Known for their resilience and teamwork, Wales is supported by a dedicated fan base and a strong domestic league. The team is determined to make a mark at the 2025 World Cup, showcasing their skills and spirit on the international stage.

Wales will play key matches at the Principality Stadium, one of the most iconic rugby venues in the world, adding to the excitement and atmosphere of the tournament.
