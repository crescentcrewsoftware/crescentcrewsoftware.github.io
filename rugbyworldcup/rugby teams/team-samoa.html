Samoa

Samoa's women's rugby team is known for its strength, heart, and Pacific Island flair. The team has made significant progress in recent years, competing in regional and international tournaments. Samoa's players are celebrated for their physicality, teamwork, and commitment to the sport.

With a growing rugby culture and strong support from fans, Samoa is ready to make an impact at the 2025 World Cup. The team is focused on inspiring the next generation of female rugby players in the Pacific region.
