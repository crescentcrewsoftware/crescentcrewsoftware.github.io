South Africa

South Africa's women's rugby team, the Springbok Women, has shown remarkable growth and determination in recent years. Competing in the World Cup and continental tournaments, South Africa is known for its physical style of play and strong forward pack. The team is committed to inspiring the next generation of female rugby players in Africa and beyond.

With a focus on development and inclusivity, South Africa continues to build its presence on the world stage. The team is supported by a passionate rugby community and is eager to make a strong impact at the 2025 World Cup.
