Women's Rugby World Cup 2025

About the Tournament

The Women's Rugby World Cup 2025 will be the tenth edition of the premier international competition in women's rugby union. Hosted in England, the tournament will feature the best teams from around the world competing for the prestigious trophy.

About the Venues

Twickenham Stadium

The largest dedicated rugby stadium in the world, Twickenham is the home of English rugby and will host the opening match and final. Its iconic atmosphere makes it a fitting stage for the Women's Rugby World Cup 2025.

Ashton Gate

Located in Bristol, Ashton Gate is a modern venue known for its passionate crowds and excellent facilities. It will host several key matches during the tournament.

St James' Park

Newcastle's St James' Park is a historic stadium with a vibrant atmosphere. It will be a highlight venue for the Women's Rugby World Cup, hosting top international clashes.

Principality Stadium

Located in Cardiff, Wales, the Principality Stadium is famous for its retractable roof and electric rugby atmosphere. It will host several matches, including knockout rounds.

Stadium of Light

Sunderland's Stadium of Light is a modern venue with a rich sporting history. It will welcome teams and fans from around the world for the Women's Rugby World Cup.

Old Trafford

Manchester's Old Trafford is one of the UK's most famous stadiums. Its large capacity and world-class facilities make it a prime venue for the tournament.
