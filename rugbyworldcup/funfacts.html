Fun Facts

  • The first Women's Rugby World Cup was held in Wales in 1991.
  • New Zealand has won the most titles in tournament history (6 times).
  • England last hosted the Women's Rugby World Cup in 2010.
  • The 2025 edition will feature more teams and matches than ever before.
  • Women's rugby is one of the fastest growing sports globally.
  • Canada and USA are the only North American teams to reach the final.
  • France has reached the semi-finals more than any other European team.
