The Rules Of Basketball

Only five players per team on the court.

Score more than your opponent to win.

Score within the shot clock.

Dribbling advances the ball.

The offense has five seconds to inbound the ball.

offense must advance the ball.

and ballhandler must remain inbounds.

can’t interfere with a shot on a downward trajectory.

Each team can have a maximum of 5 players on the court at any one time.

The ball can only be moved by either dribbling (bouncing the ball) or passing the ball.

After the ball goes into a team’s half and they win possession back the ball must then make it back over the half way line within 10 seconds.
